Vin Diesel &quot;Dad Bod&quot; Photos: Actor Criticizes Body Shamers

Vin Diesel took to Instagram on Sunday to shame the body shamers who claimed that the famously fit actor had lost his six pack — and gained a dad bod — after pictures surfaced of him hanging out on his balcony in Miami last week.

In the first post, Diesel shared a picture of himself dressed in a black T-shirt and jeans, looking muscular and trim. The action star had spent the day promoting his new film The Witch Hunter, and he captioned the pic: “It is amazing the response from the journalists who I have been talking to for the last two days in New York. Today one wanted to see the dad bod. Haha,”I am wondering if I should show the picture… Body-shaming is always wrong! What do you think?”

And in the second post on Sunday, Diesel lifted his t-shirt to reveal his toned stomach. “The pic from yesterday…,” he wrote in the caption. “For those who wanted the show… For all the angels that love dad bod regardless…”
