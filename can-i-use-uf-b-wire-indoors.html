Simply put, what is UF B wire used for?

Copper Building Wire › UF-B Wire (Direct Burial) (Direct Burial) UF-B wire is often used as an underground feeder cable to deliver electricity from an existing building to outside lighting fixtures, pumps, sheds and other outdoor equipment. The cable may also be utilized as direct burial cable.

Subsequently, question is, can you put UF B in conduit? Running UF wire via metal or PVC conduit. Moreover, can you utilize outside wire indoors?

While the electrical wire designated for outdoor usage may be used within, it would be harmful to utilize interior cables outdoors. Indoor wire is meant for usage inside the home and is not equipped to manage the environmental elements present outside such as temperature, moisture and even chemicals in certain circumstances.

 

What is the difference between UF and UF B Wire?

Type UF B cable is made for direct burial underground without the use of conduit or other piping, and is also gray in color and is marked as type UF B cable to denote the difference between it and the regular type UF cable, which is not suitable for direct burial outside of conduit or the proper piping material.
