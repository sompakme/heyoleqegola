Ubi Franklin's estranged baby mama Sandra Iheuwa shares photo of ex-hubby and their 2 kids

- Sandra Iheuwa, Ubi Franklin's estranged baby mama, recently opened up on another side of her life

- The female entrepreneur shared a photo of her ex-hubby and the two kids she had with him

- Also, Sandra told her curious fans that she got married at the age of 23

Ubi Franklin's 4th baby mama Sandra Iheuwa recently put a break to talking about her baby daddy and focused on another part of her life. In an Instagram post, she shared a photo of her ex-hubby and the two daughters they had together.

In a caption, Sandra jovially referred to her ex-hubby and the girls as her family. She also revealed that she has three other stepchildren possibly fathered by her ex-husband.

Sandra who shared the photo in celebration of the 10th birthday celebration of her first daughter Isabella made many fans very curious with this big reveal that she actually did have a family before.
