Amal Clooney Glows in Emerald Green Dress for Lake Como Dinner

  • The most loved couple of the century – Amal and George Clooney – were recently spotted having dinner near their Lake Como home with family.
  • Amal wore a stunning emerald green dress, accenting the outfit with bright gold accessories.
  • George also looked cool and sharp in a charcoal gray suit and striped button-up.

If you’re going to have dinner in Northern Italy, then you might as well do it in style.

Probably no one takes that sentiment more seriously than Amal Clooney, the human rights lawyer with a sense of fashion that serves whether she's in the courtroom or on a dinner date.

BACKGRID

Clooney was recently spotted out for dinner with her mother and her husband, actor and Academy Award winner George Clooney, near their home in the romantic Italian countryside near glittering Lake Como. The group arrived in 007 fashion (via boat) to the five-star luxury hotel of Villa d'Este for dinner.

Looking elegant and poised in William Vintage's “Great Unknown Emerald Green Ribbed Silk Dress, circa 1959," Clooney accessorized the look with statement gold earrings, a quaint clutch, and gold sandals. Her hubby looked equally stylish as he strutted through the villa in a charcoal gray suit and a relaxed button-up.
