Cody Rhodes Remains Prominent on Rokus AEW Dynamite Splash Screen

Cody Rhodes has been enjoying a successful run since his return at WrestleMania 38. However, there seems to be a minor hiccup involving his association with AEW, which he parted ways with some time ago.

In February of the previous year, Cody Rhodes made a surprising exit from AEW, leaving fans in astonishment. Before his departure, he had played a pivotal role in AEW, contributing to the growth of the promotion and helping elevate several talents with his impressive in-ring performances.

It’s important to note that Cody Rhodes is no longer associated with AEW. However, if you search for “AEW Dynamite” on Roku, a streaming platform, you may still encounter Cody Rhodes on the splash screen. This has left some fans puzzled, as it appears that Roku may not be keeping up with the latest developments in the wrestling world.

Interestingly, Cody Rhodes is set to make significant announcement on NXT in the near future, a show that will compete directly with AEW Dynamite. It remains to be seen what this announcement will entail and how it will impact both wrestling promotions.
