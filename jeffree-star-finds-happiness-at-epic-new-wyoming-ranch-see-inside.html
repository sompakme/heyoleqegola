Jeffree Star 'finds happiness' at epic new Wyoming ranch see inside

The YouTuber purchased the home in December 2020

    Jeffree Star purchased an epic 70-acre ranch in Wyoming at the end of 2020, and although it is practically the polar opposite of his former pink mansion in Calabasas, he is certainly happy with the move.

    Speaking on a recent YouTube video, he said he had "found a lot more happiness" in Casper, Wyoming. "It's so peaceful and quiet here," he added. "My view of the mountain is unreal. So far, I have accumulated 500 acres of peace and quiet and I am working on some more, but we have a beautiful home here and I am going to build a future here." He will, however, split each year between his new house and his main home in the Hidden Hills neighbourhood of California.
