Canadian Goose

Lil Uzi Vert just surprised fans with a dope little project this Friday afternoon. The rapper took to Soundcloud to drop Lil Uzi Vert Vs. The World, which is filled with heat, including production from Don Cannon, Metro Boomin and Wondagurl. However one of the stand-outs, on first listen, is actually the project's opener, "Canadian Goose," produced by lesser-known Maaly Raw. 

It's the energetic type of trap banger that Uzi does so well, all about how he needs to cop himself some "Canadian Goose," possibly in reference to the Canada Goose brand, a high-quality (and high-price) company that's been known to collaborate with Drake's OVO among others-- meaning, in general, that Uzi is cold as fuck, and he only messes with top-notch shit.

The animated cover art features the purple-haired Lil Uzi doing his favorite emoji's eye-roll stance. The project is expected to be released on iTunes. Stream the full project here.
