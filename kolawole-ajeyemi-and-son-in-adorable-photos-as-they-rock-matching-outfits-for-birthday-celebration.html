Kolawole Ajeyemi and Son In Adorable Photos as they Rock Matching Outfits for Birthday Celebration

  • Actor Kolawole Ajeyemi took to his Instagram page to share adorable photos of him with his son, Ire, in matching outfits
  • To the delight of his fans, he posted the images on Friday, August 13, as Ire celebrates his second birthday anniversary
  • Captioning the pictures, Ajeyemi said the bond he has with Ire cannot be compared with any other thing

Nollywood actor, Kolawole Ajeyemi no doubt has an admirable bond with his child, Ire, whom his wife and actress Toyin Abraham, has with him.

In a series of pictures on his Instagram page, Ajeyemi noted that a bond between a son and his father is paramount to any other, as he shared pictures of him with Ire on his second birthday.

Rocking matching outfits to mark the birthday celebration, Ajeyemi posted pictures of him with his son in blue stripe suits that were complemented with sparkling white shirts underneath.
