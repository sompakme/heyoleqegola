Pisces Season Nail Colors for Each Zodiac Sign

Every year, Pisces season runs from February 18 to March 20 — which, in turn, marks the beginning of spring. With the ingress of the sun into the dreamy and imaginative spirit of Pisces, many of us will want to polish our nails with soft hues made with metallics, pearlescent finishes, and sparkles. Pisces nails are all about fantasy and glamour.

These enchanting accents will add charm to our fingertips and enrich our souls as we embrace the celestial loveliness of Pisces season. The best part? Your don't have to be a Piscean to pull off these inspired nail colors.

To celebrate Pisces season, we've rounded up the best nail polish shade for each zodiac sign to try out. From chartreuse for the extravagant Leo and deep teal for Sagittarius to a beautiful lavender for innovative Aquarius — there's a perfect lacquer for everyone.
