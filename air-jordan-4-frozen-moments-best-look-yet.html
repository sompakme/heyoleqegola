Air Jordan 4 &quot;Frozen Moments&quot; Best Look Yet

When the Air Jordan 4 came out back in 1989, it definitely stopped people in their tracks. Overall, many felt like Tinker Hatfield had outdone himself with the Jordan 4. However, this shoe was a bit of an improvement. If you liked the Jordan 3 but didn't care for the elephant print, then these were a massive improvement. Ultimately, this sneaker is still beloved to this day. Consequently, Jordan Brand has felt emboldened to drop new colorways. Unsurprisingly, fans have continued to cop them.

Throughout 2023, we have already seen quite a few Air Jordan 4 models make their way to the market. Furthermore, many more have been teased by the brand. This has been an amazing prospect for fans as they will now be able to grow their collections. Not to mention, some dope women's exclusives are on the horizon. Among them is this "Frozen Moments" color scheme, found below.

Image via JD Sports

Firstly, this sneaker can be categorized by its mostly grey upper. The grey is made with a mix of materials, from leather to suede. Moreover, there are some white laces here that add a pop of color. Lastly, the sides possess some silver, that truly makes this shoe flash. Overall, it is a pretty solid colorway for all months of the year. We're sure other fans would agree with that statement.

More Photos

According to Sneaker Bar Detroit, rumor has it that these kicks will be released on August 26th for $225 USD. Although some stores have set their release calendars accordingly, there is always a chance for these to change. Either way, these are shaping up to be a fantastic women's exclusive.
