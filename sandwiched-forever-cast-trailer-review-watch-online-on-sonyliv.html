Sandwiched Forever: Cast, Trailer, Review , Watch Online On SonyLiv

“Sandwiched Forever,” a Hindi web series, made its grand debut on SonyLIV on December 25, 2020. Directed by the renowned Rohan Sippy, this show brings a breath of fresh air, steering away from the usual crime dramas dominating the digital series landscape. Penned by Devand Kakkad, with the delightful music and sound crafted by Pankaj Awasti, it offers a comedic escape available in both Hindi and English.

The Story of ‘Sandwiched Forever’

Meet Sameer (Kunal Roy Kapur) and Naina (Aahana Kumra), a young couple with dreams of blissful independence in their marriage. However, their plans take a hilarious turn when both sets of parents decide to move in next door, keeping a close eye on them. Naina, a badminton player, and Sameer, a freelance game developer, navigate through daily chaos, sprinkled with cheeky comments from their in-laws. The show weaves a tale that is both comical and poignant, capturing the essence of the young couple’s entertaining struggles.

    • Review: A Perfect Blend of Comedy and Drama

    Released as a Christmas treat, “Sandwiched Forever” has quickly become a binge-watcher’s delight. With a perfect mix of comedy, drama, and suspense, it stands out in the crowded web series landscape. The stellar cast adds a touch of glamour to each episode, making it a lighthearted comedy perfect for a watch with close friends. However, be cautious about viewing with relatives due to some mature content. While it may not reach the comedic heights of classics like Sarabhai Vs Sarabhai and Dekh Bhai Dekh, it certainly delivers as a delightful one-time watch.

    Conclusion: A Comedy Worth Savoring

    In the realm of Indian web series, “Sandwiched Forever” emerges as a standout, offering hearty laughs and relatable chaos. The stellar cast, engaging storyline, and a dash of suspense make it a perfect choice for those seeking a light-hearted escape into the world of family comedy. With its unique blend of humor and drama, ‘Sandwiched Forever’ promises to be a delightful addition to your binge-watch list on SonyLIV.
