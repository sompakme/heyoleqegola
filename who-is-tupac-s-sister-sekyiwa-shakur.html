Who is Tupac's sister, Sekyiwa Shakur?

SEKYIWA Shakur is the sister of hip-hop legend Tupac, who died tragically on September 13, 1996.

While Sekyiwa Shakur continues her brother Tupac's legacy of fighting for equality and justice, some fans are curious to know more about her personal life.

Who is Tupac's sister, Sekyiwa Shakur?

Sekyiwa shared the same mother as Tupac - Afeni Shakur - while her dad was the star's stepfather Mutulu.

She and Tupac grew up together in Harlem, New York, but she has made a point of staying out of the spotlight.

Who was arrested in relation to Tupac's death?

Gangster Keefe D was arrested on September 29, 2023, in relation to Tupac's death after he talked about committing the crime.

Keefe has claimed multiple times that he was riding shotgun in a white Cadillac and offered his nephew, Orlando Anderson, the handgun that delivered the fatal shots to the Death Row rapper.

Tupac was shot near the Las Vegas strip in 1996.

The arrest comes just months after investigators raided a home in Las Vegas that was connected to Keefe.

On January 9, 2024, Keefe's bail was set to $750,000.
