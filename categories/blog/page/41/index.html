Annaleigh Ashford wants you to remember the impact of Paula Jones. The actress and Broadway star plays the former Arkansas state employee in Ryan Murphy's latest iteration of American Crime Story, titled Impeachment, in which President Bill Clinton's 1998 sex scandal—and the women involved—take center stage. Jones was a major figure in the investigative case and the media circus that followed. The first woman to accuse the former president of sexual misconduct, she and her accusations only gained the spotlight when news of the president's inappropriate affair with former White House intern Monica Lewinsky came to light.
