What is Kentavious Caldwell-Pope's Net Worth as of 2023?

Kentavious attended Greenville High School in Greenville, Georgia. He began playing basketball for his high school and averaged 31 points and 8.2 rebounds in his junior year. As a senior, Kentavious played in the Jordan Brand Classic and the 2011 McDonald’s All-American Boys Game. He ranked third among the nation’s top shooting guards in his senior year.

In 2011, Kentavious committed to play for the University of Georgia. As a freshman, Kentavious was selected to play in the Coaches’ Freshman All-SEC Team. This team included many NBA stars, including Anthony Davis, Bradley Beal, and Michael Kidd-Gilchrist.

As a sophomore, Kentavious Caldwell-Pope averaged 18.5 points and 7.1 rebounds per game and was honoured with the Southeastern Conference Men’s Basketball Player of the Year award. He recorded a career-high 32 points and 13 rebounds in a match against the LSU Tigers men’s basketball team. After this season, Kentavious declared for the 2013 NBA draft.
