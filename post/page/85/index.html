Julius Roberts Plum and fig leaf jam recipe

published on September 14, 2024
Plums make a great jam, the fruit collapsing into an oozing texture, with a welcome tartness and vibrant colour. But the addition of fig leaves is really quite special. They have a floral, almost buttery and almondy aroma, the flavour of which goes beautifully with the plums.  When I’m making jam, I use a ratio of 1kg of fruit to 350-500g of pectin or jamming sugar.
