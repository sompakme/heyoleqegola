The Clooney Foundation for Justice is starting 2024 with a new executive leadership team. The team is charged with leading CFJ’s mission to provide free legal support and advocacy to victims of genocide, unlawful imprisonment and other human rights abuses. The appointments are said to be driven by CFJ’s co-founders George and Amal Clooney, who have guided the foundation from inception seven years ago into an organization with influence now in more than 40 countries.
