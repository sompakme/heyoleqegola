Is Loose leaf better than tea bags?

published on September 17, 2024
Flavor profile: Loose-leaf tea offers a stronger flavor profile than bagged tea because the tea leaves tend to be fresher, and less ground-down. Also, tea bags separate the warm water from the leaves, which can dull the flavor of the tea. Is Loose leaf tea healthier than tea bags? If you drink tea to enjoy the health benefits, loose leaf tea may be a better choice.
