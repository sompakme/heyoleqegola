Abisola Omole, or Abimarvel as the majority of her community refer to her as, is a London-based creative and CEO. Starting her career back in 2008 by creating a fashion and lifestyle blog, Abisola shortly after went on to contribute to titles that included MSN & Grazia, whilst studying at University of The Arts London. She then went on to launch The Apārtment in 2012, which has since developed into a digital and creative agency that today, now specialises in event production/design as well as digital and brand strategy. In 2017, the development of three further companies, arva, studio arva, and fole studio began. All four companies sit under The Apārtment Global Group (TĀGG) umbrella. Abisola is CEO of the group and also holds the positions of Creative Director at arva, Brand Director at Cube Collective, a 360 digital atelier that specialises in social media strategy and brand communication, and is Beauty Editor at Large of CUBICLE, a printed book series and digital storytelling platform. Abi contributes content on the plus-size fashion space for BAZAAR.com.
