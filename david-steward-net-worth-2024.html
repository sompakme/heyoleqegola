David Steward’s Early Life and Career

David Steward was born in Chicago, Illinois, and raised in Clinton, Missouri. He faced numerous challenges growing up, including racial discrimination and poverty. Steward’s entrepreneurial journey began in 1990 when he co-founded World Wide Technology. World Wide Technology is the primary source of Steward’s wealth. The company has consistently grown over the years, with revenues exceeding $13 billion in 2020. As the chairman and co-founder, Steward owns a significant stake in the company, contributing to his substantial net worth.
