Katherine Heigl Flaunts Her Growing Baby Bump While Out to Lunch with Husband Josh Kelley

Pregnancy sure looks good on Katherine Heigl. On Monday, the mom-to-be glowed while dressing her growing baby bump in radiant white as she enjoyed a casual lunch date in Los Angeles with her husband Josh Kelley.

The 37-year-old kept cool in the California heat in a white blouse-inspired T-shirt featuring lace detailing in the front, which she paired with distressed jeans with rolled up hems and tan flip-flops for an effortlessly cute and comfy ensemble.

The expectant star, who recently revealed she is pregnant for the first time with a baby boy, topped off her casual look with a brown side bag, a white pendant necklace, and oversize sunglasses. She pulled her short blonde hair back from her face in a half-up style to draw attention to her radiant complexion.

Heigl's other half also went simple for the pair's lunch date, opting for a black polo, gray shorts, and gray socks worn with green slip-on shoes.
