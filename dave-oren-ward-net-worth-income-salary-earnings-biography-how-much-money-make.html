Dave Oren Ward was an actor from the United States. Ward is most recognised for his roles in films such as Witchouse, My First Name Spelled Backwards, The Killer Eye, and Pariah, among other titles. Ward was slain in a three-on-one street brawl in April 1999 in Hollywood, California, while under the influence of alcohol. Following a disagreement, Ward and two companions engaged in a fight with a lone guy, during which both Ward and the other man were stabbed. Ward was taken to a hospital where he died a few hours later.
