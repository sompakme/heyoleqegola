Floyd Mayweather Sr. Reportedly Tied The Knot In Las Vegas

According to TMZ Sports, the 65-year old former boxer reportedly tied the knot at a private ceremony in Las Vegas back in February, but the photos have only just hit the 'net. TMZ reports, "sources connected to the situation tell us ... Floyd and Lois Ann Roberts hit up the Stained Glass Wedding Chapel in Vegas on February 4 and swapped "I Dos" in a very quick and quiet ceremony." Although the Stained Glass Wedding Chapel offers Elvis-themed weddings, it is believed Floyd Sr. went the more traditional route.

As for his son, undefeated boxing legend Floyd Mayweather Jr., TMZ reports that he was not in attendance for the wedding, but he is in full support of his father's marriage to Lois Ann Roberts. "They've known each other for decades and he considered her family even before things became official."
