Catherine Tyldesleys husband Tom Pitfield set to star in new TV show

    Catherine Tyldesley has proved herself as a talented actress, and has been well-known to fans across the country as beloved barmaid Eva Price since appearing in the soap back in 2011. But it looks like there's another star in the family, as her husband, Tom Pitfield, is set to make his own mark in the world of entertainment this summer, launching his own TV career as a presenter on new ITV show . Tom, a freelance fitness consultant, will give his expertise on how to lead a healthy lifestyle, alongside the show’s host, Ranvir Singh, chef Dale Pinnock and money expert Gemma Godfrey.

    Catherine congratulated Tom on his new TV role over on Instagram 

    Proud wife Catherine took to Instagram to congratulate her husband on his new role. Sharing a photo of the Eat, Shop, Save’s promotional image, the 33-year-old wrote: “ #proudwife klaxon!!!! Can't wait to see this!! Featuring my talented, awesome hubby @pitfield_t and my lovely friend and nutrition guru @themedicinalchef . This is gonna be ace!! These guys know there stuff!”

    EXCLUSIVE STORY: Catherine Tyldesley marries Tom Pitfield

    Catherine married Tom back in 2016 in a star-studded HELLO! wedding at Colshaw Hall in Cheshire. At the time, the star spoke of her happiness on her big day, saying: "It was my dream come true. I had been looking forward to this moment for so long. Tom and I are very traditional and it was important for us to show our deep commitment to each other."

    The couple are the proud parents to two-year-old Alfie, and recently enjoyed taking their little boy to Dubai, where they spent their honeymoon last year. Talking exclusively to HELLO!, Catherine said of their special holiday: “It’s is lovely to come back here with Alfie. When he was born, Tom and I said, 'This doesn't change a thing; we still want to see the world.' And we want Alfie to see the world with us."
