Spoiler Lineup For 1/5 WWE New Years Revolution SmackDown

WWE SmackDown is gearing up to kick off 2024 with a big episode. Vancouver will host New Years Revolution, and now we have the spoiler line-up.

Sean Sapp noted a list of spoilers behind Fightful’s paywall. This included a spoiler lineup, so fans know what to expect, and in which order, tonight for SmackDown.

WWE SmackDown New Year’s Revolution Spoiler (1/5/24) Lineup:

  • The final showdown in the US Tournament Contender sees Kevin Owens pitted against Santos Escobar.
  • Lashley and the Street Profits participate in a promotional segment.
  • IYO SKY and Michin square off for the WWE Women’s Title.
  • Butch teams up with an undisclosed partner to battle Pretty Deadly (Spoiler Alert).
  • A top contender match features LA Knight going head-to-head with AJ Styles and Randy Orton.

WWE SmackDown New Year’s Revolution (1/5/24) Backstage News:

  • Charles Robinson officiates the main event.Jessika Carr serves as the referee for the Women’s title match.
  • A pre-show dark match is slated with Gable Steveson taking on Cedric Alexander.
  • Following the event, there is a post-show dark match featuring Bayley against Chelsea Green.
  • Angel Garza and Humberto Carrillo are on the show’s schedule.

WWE SmackDown New Year’s Revolution (1/5/24) Spoilers:

  • The top contender match is planned to span three segments.
  • The US Title Tourney match is allocated three segments.
  • AOP, Paul Ellering, Karrion Kross, and Scarlett are all scheduled to make a return in a segment involving Lashley and the Street Profits.
  • Tyler Bate is geared up for his Smackdown debut as Butch’s partner.
  • The Women’s Title match is scheduled to occupy two segments.
  • Nick Aldis is expected to be present at ringside for the main event.

We will have to see how things pan out when everything airs live on SmackDown. This should be quite a show, with tons of big news stories coming out of the two-hour broadcast.
