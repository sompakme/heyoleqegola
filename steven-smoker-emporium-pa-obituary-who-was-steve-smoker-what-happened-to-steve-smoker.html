Steve Smoker was raised in The Bronx of New York. Steve truly found his calling in nightlife and hospitality, where he became a fixture on both New York’s lounge scene as well as Florida’s. Steve wasn’t just a lounge owner and manager; he was an all-encompassing personality, often bartending, managing, or bouncing as needed. His impressive ability to balance multiple duties gracefully made him an esteemed member of the nightlife scene; those fortunate to know Steve fondly remember his legacy with laughter, warmth, and genuine camaraderie.
