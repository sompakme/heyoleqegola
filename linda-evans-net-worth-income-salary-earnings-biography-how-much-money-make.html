Linda Evans (born November 18, 1942) is an American actress best known for her work on television. She was born into a family of actors. She originally garnered national attention in the 1960s as Audra Barkley on the Western television series The Big Valley (1965–1969), in which she starred. The part of Krystle Carrington on the ABC prime time soap series Dynasty in the 1980s, which she portrayed from 1981 to 1989, is, however, her most well-known role.
