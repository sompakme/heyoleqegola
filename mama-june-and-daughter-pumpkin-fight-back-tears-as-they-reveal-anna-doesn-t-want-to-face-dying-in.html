MAMA June and Pumpkin sat down for an emotional interview about Anna Cardwell.

The reality television star passed away on 9th December 2023 just seven months after she was diagnosed with adrenal carcinoma.

Mama June and her daughters, Lauryn Efird aka Pumpkin, Alana Thompson, and Jessica Shannon, have been devastated since the tragic news.

Pumpkin and Mama June sat down for en exclusive interview to reveal Anna's private battle in the months before her passing.

A preview clip was released for the upcoming show Mama June: Family Crisis, which followed the star's family life during Anna's cancer battle.

The mother and daughter claimed that Anna did not face her diagnosis after it was given.

It was recently announced that Mama June is set to appear in court this month against Michael for the custody of Kylee.

The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed that June and Michael will be in court for the first time in their nasty battle over Kaitlyn, which began just days after Anna's tragic passing in December from adrenal cancer at just 29 years old.

In earlier filings, Caleb waived his rights to be involved in the proceedings over the 11-year-old.
