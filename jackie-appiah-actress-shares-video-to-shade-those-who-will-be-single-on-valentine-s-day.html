Jackie Appiah: Actress Shares Video to Shade Those Who Will Be Single on Valentine's Day

  • Jackie Appiah, in a recent video, threw shades at those who would be single on Valentine's Day
  • The actress, using the viral Valentine's Day sound "Valentine is coming" was seen hilariously miming and dancing
  • Jackie Appiah, a celebrated Ghanaian actress, got reactions from netizens on her recent act

Valentine's Day, the celebration of love and affection, is just around the corner, and Ghanaian actress Jackie Appiah has taken a playful jab at those who may find themselves flying solo on the day.

Jackie Appiah, in a recent video uploaded to her TikTok page, poked all those who would be single with the popular Valentine's Day anthem, "Valentine is coming."

The actress showcased her humour and wit, sending a lighthearted message to all the singles out there.
