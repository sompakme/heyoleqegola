Everlane Black Friday Sale 2023: Our Favorite Deals

Every year when Black Friday and Cyber Monday roll around, there are a few brands I just know that I’m going to shop. These are the brands I browse during the remaining 11 months of the year, making a list of pieces that I’m going to invest in when prices plummet after Thanksgiving. One of the brands topping my list year after year? Everlane. The sustainable brand has become known across the internet for its amazing Black Friday and Cyber Monday sales, so it’s only fair that I share the best deals with you.

When is the Everlane Sale 2023?

Everlane's sale officially began on Tuesday, November 21 and will run through Monday, November 27 (a.k.a. Cyber Monday). 

What's In Everlane's Sale

The brand is offering up to 50 percent off select styles from their Perform and Track collections, as well as their best-in-class sweaters, teddy fleeces, and their ReNew outerwear and denim ranges. Better yet, Everlane is continuing its Black Friday Fund—the initiative has raised over 1.5 million dollars since launching in 2014. This year, they're teaming up with the New Zealand Merino Company, the world's leading responsible and regenerative wool supplier: Every $100 spent will kickstart regenerative grazing for 66 sheep.
