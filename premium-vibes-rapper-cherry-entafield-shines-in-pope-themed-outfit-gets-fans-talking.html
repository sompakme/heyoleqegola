&quot;Premium Vibes&quot;: Rapper Cherry Entafield Shines in Pope-Themed Outfit, Gets Fans Talking

  • Rapper Cherry Entafield wore a Pope-themed outfit that looked regal on her and got her fans to react in different ways
  • The outfit comprised a yellow robe with colourful embellishments, and her cap also spoke of royalty
  • Her attire wowed her fans as they admired her, with some making nice comments while others dropped love and fire emojis on her post

Nigerian rapper Cherry Entafield has shown what it feels like to be a spiritual being in a human body as she adorned a Pope-like outfit for a photo shoot.

She looked radiant as she gave different poses in front of the camera.

Her yellow robe flowed to the ground and was styled with colourful embellishments. She wore gold and orange accessories on her fingers and ears.

Just like Pope Francis of the Catholic church dresses, the entertainer added a cap to her outfit. She also held a gold staff that complemented her look.
