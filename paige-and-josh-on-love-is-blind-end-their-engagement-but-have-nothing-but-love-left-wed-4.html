Paige and Josh on Love is Blind end their engagement but have nothing but love left Wed 4

Season 5 spoilers – Paige Tillman and Josh Simmons on Love is Blind barely had their romance shown on Netflix. However, we’re here to spill the tea on how they got engaged but then ended things. Fans are super confused over their engagement announcement – which revealed they didn’t last.

Love is Blind sees total strangers date but without the physical part of seeing each other in the flesh. That moment doesn’t come until an engagement has taken place through the Netflix pods. We’ve got the latest update on Paige and Josh from season 5 after a confusing Instagram post.

Paige and Josh on Love is Blind

Paige and Josh immediately liked each other after speaking in the pods. Paige is a 32-year-old stylist who is considered by her peers as the nicest person in the room, while Josh, also 32, is a sales representative.

He said, “I want to give my partner what the life they deserve.” She went on the show looking to find someone who “loves the way [she] loves” and the two went on to develop a strong bond.

They were mainly featured in episode 1 of Love is Blind season 5. Yet, they only had eyes for each other throughout the season but they didn’t quite make it to the altar.

Season 5 spoilers – They got engaged

Paige revealed some news! She wrote: “We got engaged. Josh and I are so excited to announce we found love in the pods. We were each other’s first pod dates and number one picks from the beginning.”

Fans are devastated that they didn’t see Paige and Josh’s love story play out. “Our story is filled with love, beautiful moments, and happiness with no drama,” penned the Netflix stars.

Paige and Josh on Love is Blind season 5 spoilers reveal that they were together for a year after the show. Josh said: “It’s unfortunate what stories they decided to follow but my story will be told soon.”

They later ended their engagement

After the show, they stayed engaged and even planned their wedding. However, the more time they shared together, they “fell deeply in love” and found out they wanted different things out of life.

Paige said: “We made the hardest decision to end our engagement, we have nothing but love and respect for each other and always will. We are so grateful LIB brought us together and would do it all over again.”

One fan commented, “Most of us really thought you’d end up with Izzy 😅.” Paige replied and wrote: “Plot twist.” Another penned: “This seems like a ‘shouldn’t break up post though’.”
