Dior Cruise 2024 with Top Gun's Monica Barbaro

Sitting front row at the Dior Cruise 2024 fashion show would be a monumental occasion for any fashion fan. But for Monica Barbaro, the breakout star of last year's Top Gun: Maverick, attending the show was particularly sentimental. Not only was it her first-ever fashion show, but it was also held in Mexico City, a city that holds a special place in her heart.

Ahead, Barbaro shares more about the meaning behind the event, plus all about her getting-ready experience.

InStyle: How does it feel to be attending Dior Cruise show in Mexico City?

Monica Barbaro: First. Fashion. Show! I’m beyond excited. My grandmother was from this city and she loved fashion. I like to think she would have enjoyed seeing the way Maria Grazia Chiuri brought Dior [here] with intrigue and elegance.

What was the inspiration for your look at the show?

I’ve always thought a fashion show would be a perfect time to push the envelope so I was excited by the idea of wearing something that didn’t look like anything I’d ever worn. I wanted something cool but also comfortable that would evoke a creative spirit and this beautiful, funky coat felt just right.

What products are you bringing in your bag?

Dior Addict Lipstick in shade Burgundy Oblique, Dior Forever Cushion Powder… also my little 35mm camera with plenty of film.

What would we be surprised to know about your getting-ready process?

I tend to over-caffeinate out of sheer excitement.

Getting Glam


This was my second time getting to work with Ana G de V [for makeup] and Erick Moreno [for hair]. It’s always nice to get to collaborate with the same talented artists you’ve already worked with in a city away from home.
