FAQs

Does Brooks Brothers have free shipping?

Standard shipping is free for orders of at least $200. If you’re a VIP-level customer at Brooks Brothers, you can also get free 2-day shipping on each order. Usually, standard shipping starts from $5.95.

How do I get a 15% off email offer from Brooks Brothers?

Sign up for the Brooks Brothers newsletter with your preferred email address to get 15% off your first order. Simply go to the Brooks Brothers website and click ‘Get 15% off’ and type your email address. You’ll also be kept updated on the latest sales, products, and promotions.  

Does Brooks Brothers have a military discount?

Brooks Brothers has a military discount in-store only. Unfortunately, there is no online military discount at this time. Our team regularly updates this page with the best offers; we’ll let you know if this changes.

Does Brooks Brothers have a student discount?

There are no student discounts at Brooks Brothers, as of writing. However, you can still save on your Brooks Brothers order with one of our promo codes. You can also sign up for their emails and get 15% off your next order.  

When are the Brooks Brothers sales?

Brooks Brothers has sales throughout the year on their ‘Sale’ tab. We’ve seen previous discounts of up to 70% off in their Clearance section. You can save on your shirts, blazers, or dresses with the sale.  

What is the return policy at Brooks Brothers?

You can return your Brooks Brothers purchase within 60 days. Your order should be unworn, unwashed, and in its original condition. You cannot return final sale items.

Whether you’re looking for a Brooks Brothers suit or casual shirt, you’ll find discounts in the Sale. Each product is the same high-quality material simply moved to the sale due to seasonality. You’ll still be shopping premium clothing, simply at a price suited for a smaller budget.

There’s also the choice to shop by product type, such as polos or pants. This sale is available throughout the year, so you can pick up that red fleece or merino wool suit for less.

MyBrooks Rewards - Join the MyBrooks Rewards program to start earning points on your Brooks Brothers orders. This is completely free to sign up for and you’ll get 2 points for every $1 you spend. With each tier, you will get more points and extra rewards too such as free shipping.
