Real-LifeGame of Thrones CoupleKit Harington and Rose Leslie Enjoy a N.Y.C. Da

Jon Snow and Ygritte’s love affair might have ended on Game of Thrones, but Kit Harington and Rose Leslie are still going strong in real life. The doomed love interests turned IRL couple stepped out together in N.Y.C. last week looking more in love than ever.

Harington and Leslie went for a stroll hand-in-hand through Manhattan, and they couldn’t seem to take their eyes off of each other. Even when navigating the Big Apple’s busy streets, Harington looked lovingly on his girlfriend, who looked chic in a black maxi and patent leather loafers.

Game of Thrones’s resurrected leading man didn’t look too shabby himself. The star pulled back his infamous dark curls into a pony, throwing on a dark tee, cuffed blue khakis, and lace-up black dress shoes.

The smitten former co-stars have recently opened up about their romance, revealing that they fell in love while filming Game of Thrones in Iceland.
