Tyler, The Creator Announces World Tour

With Cherry Bomb physical copies finally out after a delay, Tyler, The Creator is ready to take his show on the road. Starting on May 1st (tomorrow), he'll be touring the world in support of the wild new project.

The monthlong trek begins in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, hits the Carolinas, Virginia and Ohio before hopping the pond and covering the UK, Germany, Switzerland, France, Spain, The Netherlands and Denmark. It'll be a busy month for Tyler, as he's doing 21 shows in 28 days. Be sure to cop Cherry Bomb and if you're still making up your mind, take a look at our review.
