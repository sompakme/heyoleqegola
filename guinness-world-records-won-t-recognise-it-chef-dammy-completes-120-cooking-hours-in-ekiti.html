Guinness World Records Wont Recognise It: Chef Dammy Completes 120 Cooking Hours in Ekiti

  • A Nigerian lady who is now popularly known as Chef Dammy has completed her cooking challenge of 120 hours
  • The lady gradually rose to fame as people debated the appropriateness of her challenge following Hilda Baci's recently completed cookathon
  • As Chef Dammy finished the challenge, her fans at the cooking venue gathered around her to celebrate

A Nigerian chef, Damilola Adeparusi, who started a 120-hour cooking task days ago, has finally completed the challenge.

Despite the mixed reactions her cooking challenge got in the face of Guinness World Records certifying Hilda Baci's record on Tuesday, June 13, the lady kept cooking.

Chef Dammy cooks for 120 hours

During her task, some dignitaries, like Professor AS Fasina, the vice-chancellor of Federal University Oye Ekiti, recognised her.
