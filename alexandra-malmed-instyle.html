Alexandra Malmed - InStyle

Title: Contributing Writer

Education: Parsons and Eugene Lang, The New School

Location: Los Angeles, CA and Santa Fe, NM

Expertise: Fine Jewelry, Fashion, Skincare

Alexandra is a fashion, beauty, and lifestyle writer for publications including Vogue.com, Coveteur, and InStyle, with over twelve years of editorial experience.

Highlights:

  • Alexandra contributes to publications including Vogue.com, Coveteur, InStyle, Man Repeller, Byrdie, Refinery29, and Los Angeles magazine.
  • Alexandra is a creative consultant and writer for emerging and established brands in fashion, beauty, wellness, fine jewelry, and travel.
  • Alexandra designs fine jewelry with her father for Tony Malmed Jewelry.

Experience

While working in contemporary art at Team Gallery, Alexandra began writing about fashion for small publications. She has now been contributing to outlets including Coveteur and Vogue.com for more than twelve years. Alexandra has been consulting and writing for private clients including Spinelli Kilcollin and VENUS ET FLEUR for more than a decade.

Alexandra also designs jewelry with her father for Tony Malmed Jewelry.

I never, ever take off my favorite pieces of jewelry. I've worn a pink sapphire ring designed by my father 24/7 for more than 21 years. Other pieces that I refuse to be without include three necklaces and a rough top Lapis ring by my father, a Spinelli Kilcollin linked ring, a gold choker that belonged to my grandmother, and my Cartier Love Bracelet.

Education

Dual degrees in Contemporary Art History and Non-Fiction Writing from Parsons and Eugene Lang, The New School, graduated with honors.
