Jonah Hill Talks Skate Culture, Mid90s, Favorite Shoes &amp; More On &quot;Sneaker Shopping&quot;

Jonah Hill, director of the new movie Mid90s, and child actor/skateboarder Sunny Suljic recently linked up with Complex's Joe La Puma for an all-new episode of "Sneaker Shopping" at Stadium Goods in NYC.

During their visit to the popular sneaker shop, the duo discussed their favorite sneakers past and present, skate culture, Adidas partnerships and the Mid90s movie, which is set in 1990s Los Angeles. The film follows Stevie, a 13-year old played by Suljic, who spends his summer navigating between his troubled home life and a group of new friends that he meets at a Motor Avenue skate shop.

At the end of the episode, Hill and Suljic rack up more than $3500 worth of gear, including some kicks that Hill plans to auction off for charity at a later date.
