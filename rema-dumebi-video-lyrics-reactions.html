Rema - Dumebi video, lyrics, reactions

Rema - Dumebi is the hottest songs in Nigeria right now. A song with a difference, swag and a great catch for anyone that gets to listen to it the first time. The lyrics and beats are just exquisite and will have you hitting the replay button.

Rema - Dumebi was released on 21st May 2019 and has been doing really well, going by the reactions and reception of fans and viewers on social media. This tune has been loved for many reasons. People seem to enjoy the fact that it is outstanding and that it has a unique concept, something rare these days. The video is especially captivating. Check out all the details you know to know about the new banger below.

Rema - Dumebi description

  • Released: 21st May 2019
  • Genre: Afrobeat
  • Length: 3:15 minutes
  • Label: Mavin Records
  • Format: Audio and video

The rising hit-maker surprised everyone with a new single that is on everyone's lips. Besides the fact that there are no women shaking their booties in the song's video, the message is delivered quite perfectly. Rema narrates a story of how he is in love with a girl that he can never own. He tries to take her away from her boyfriend as he sings to her while she and her friends are out in the field.

The quality of the video is top-notch and one that showcases Nigeria in its true natural beauty, one of the many reasons that a lot of people have taken to it. The message may be a sad one, but a lot of people can identify with it. It is very common to love someone that may never be yours. The fact that the beats are danceable is also a plus. All you need to do is master the lyrics and sing along to the tune as you dance and enjoy the excellent quality of music.
