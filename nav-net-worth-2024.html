Nav’s Early Life and Career

Nav was born and raised in Toronto, Ontario, to Punjabi parents. He developed an interest in music at a young age and began producing beats in his early teens. Nav’s career took off in 2015 when one of his songs, “Take Me Simple,” gained popularity on the streaming platform SoundCloud.

Breakthrough and Rise to Fame

In 2016, Nav collaborated with fellow Canadian rapper Drake on the song “Back to Back.” This collaboration brought him into the limelight and led to a record deal with XO and Republic Records. His debut mixtape, “Nav,” was released in 2017 and reached number four on the Billboard 200 chart.

Nav’s Musical Success and Earnings

Nav’s music has been well-received, with several of his albums and singles charting on the Billboard charts. His second studio album, “Bad Habits,” debuted at number one on the Billboard 200, demonstrating his growing popularity.

Concerts and Tours

Nav’s concerts and tours are a significant source of his income. He has performed at numerous music festivals and has headlined several tours, including the “Bad Habits Tour” in 2019.

Nav’s Other Sources of Wealth

Beyond his music, Nav has diversified his income streams. He has a clothing line, “XO,” in collaboration with The Weeknd. He also earns from endorsements and partnerships with brands.

Real Estate Investments
